# Build 93 Estimator Review Policy

IHOS output must be reviewed by a qualified estimator before use.

## Mandatory review items

- Drawing revision and addenda status.
- Quantity takeoff values.
- Production rates.
- Supplier quote inclusions and exclusions.
- Subcontract vs self-perform scope split.
- Contingency and risk allowances.
- Bonding and insurance.
- Assumptions and exclusions.
- Final bid price.

## Rule

No IHOS-generated workbook, RFQ, or bid package is final until reviewed and approved by the estimator.
