# Build 94 Supplier RFQ Policy

## Before sending an RFQ

- Confirm supplier category is correct.
- Confirm email recipient is correct.
- Confirm attachments are complete.
- Confirm quote due date is reasonable.
- Confirm scope summary does not conflict with drawings/specs.
- Include a request to redirect if the RFQ reached the wrong inbox.

## Do not send

- Incomplete drawings.
- Wrong revision drawings.
- RFQs without clear scope.
- RFQs missing bid date or quote deadline.

## Recordkeeping

- Save sent RFQ package.
- Save supplier response.
- Save quote amount, inclusions, exclusions, and contact.
