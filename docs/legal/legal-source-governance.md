# Legal source governance

The Legal Control Centre may cite only authority IDs in its code-reviewed register. Each entry has a jurisdiction, status and official URL. `active` sources may be passed to the AI service. `not_in_force`, repealed, superseded or unverified sources may be displayed for awareness but must be excluded from AI analysis.

Before production activation, management must appoint a legal-source owner and retained counsel must approve:

- the initial register and practice-area coverage;
- a recurring revalidation cadence;
- a rapid update process for legislative, regulatory and case-law change;
- evidence retained for each validation;
- the escalation process when a source is uncertain or conflicts with a contract.

Statutes are not the whole law. Contract terms, regulations, common law, project facts, municipal requirements, insurance conditions and procedural rules may change the answer. Every draft therefore identifies uncertainty and questions for counsel.
