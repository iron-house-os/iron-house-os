# Legal production activation gate

Status: **Blocked**

No production deployment or activation is authorised by Build 230.

Activation requires recorded approval from:

- Iron House management;
- retained BC construction counsel;
- privacy lead or qualified privacy adviser;
- security/release owner.

The approval package must cover access, confidentiality classification, privilege handling, approved document types, AI vendor/data terms, retention and deletion, audit access, incident response, source revalidation, contract-template control, deadline verification, backup/recovery, migration and rollback.

Release evidence must prove the feature remains administrator-only, defaults disabled, excludes inactive authorities, blocks marked personal/privileged data from AI, never verifies its own deadlines, and exposes no credential to the browser or logs.
